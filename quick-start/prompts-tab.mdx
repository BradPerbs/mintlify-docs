## Adding new Prompts

If you wish to add additional Prompts after the Onboarding prompts Configuration, you can go to the **Prompts** Tab in the left menu.

1. You can Click on the **Create Prompts** dropdown menu and select **Add Prompt** to add one prompt at a time and pick the **Language** and **Region** for it.
2. Additionally you can choose from the dropdown **Suggest Prompts** and let AI suggest additional prompts for you, then pick from the suggestions list the ones you wish to add.
4. Or you can select **Bulk Upload** prompts to upload them in bulk via CSV file.

4. Additionally at any point in time you can edit Added/imported Prompts to change their Language, Location or the prompts itself by selecting them with tickboxes on the prompt's left side.
