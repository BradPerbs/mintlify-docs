# Citations Tab

Analyze the top citations used throughout your industry.

![Citations Tab](https://i.imgur.com/RDhgFlX.gif)

## Filter Your Results

Use the **Citations** tab to see which citations are dominant in your industry. Use the filter tools to narrow down results by date range, specific tags, or models.

## Track Changes Over Time

Toggle the **Timeline View** to see how citations change on a daily basis. This ensures you are always one step ahead regarding where to direct your attention.

## Inspect Citation Links

Expand any citation to see the specific URLs that were used to generate the recommendation. Use these insights to determine which articles are driving results and to strategize where your brand should appear.
