We show you exactly what happens when people ask AI assistants about your industry, your products, and your competitors. Getting started is straightforward: just set up your company profile, add the questions your customers are asking, and let GeoGen get to work.

<Info>
  Our platform interacts with AI models the same way a person does, by using their public websites. This guarantees the data you see is authentic and truly reflects the live user experience.
</Info>

After you complete the quick setup, you’ll have your first report showing precisely how your brand is appearing across the AI landscape.

## #1 Add Your First Entity

![Add Entity Gi](/images/add-entity.gif)

- Click on **Select Entity** on the top left menu
- Select **Add New Tracking**
- Select\*\*Website \*\* (Default) or Individual (Beta)
- Fill the\*\*Website Name \*\* field with your Company/Brand Name
- Fill the **Website URL** field with your Company/Brand URL
- **Create a Description for your Company/Brand (See Instructions Below)**

  <Accordion title="AI Generated Description" defaultOpen icon="sparkles">
    Click the Sparkles Icon on at the Top-Right of the Description Field to have AI Crawl and Understand your Company/Brand and write a Description for it.

    <Note>
      Bare in mind that depending on your website structure, the Description Generated by AI may not be the most optimal for AI generated prompts afterwards, but it may be a good starting point, further tweak if necessary highlighting clearly the product/services offered by your company. 
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Manual Description" defaultOpen icon="pencil">
    <Note>
      **Instruction:**

      Enter a concise company + offering summary to improve prompt accuracy.

      **Format:**

      [COMPANY NAME] provides/offers [PRODUCT(S)/SERVICE(S)] [in REGION(S) if applicable].

      **Examples:**
      - Acme Solar provides residential solar installation and maintenance.
      - NordicFoods offers frozen vegan meals in Scandinavia.
      - ByteGuard provides cybersecurity audits and managed SOC services.
      - MedTour offers medical concierge services in the Gulf region.

      **Tip:**

      One sentence. Plain names, no slogans. Region only if it truly matters.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
